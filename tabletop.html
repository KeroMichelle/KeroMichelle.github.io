Tabletop Games

I Got You Now!

I Got You Now! is a collaborative mystery game of betrayal and destroyed trust. Mrs. Caldwell has discovered that her husband is a dirty liar and a cheat! With the suspects narrowed down to three friends, Mrs. Caldwell's objective is to expose the mistress. The non-guilty friends must help bring the mistress to justice while trying to prove their innocence. The true mistress, however, must avoid being caught and redirect suspicion to a different player!

The Academy of Magic

The Academy of Magic is a custom module for Basic Edition Dungeons & Dragons set in a magic prep school, that satirically blends traditional fantasy lore with contemporary social commentary.
